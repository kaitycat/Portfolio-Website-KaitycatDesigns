The videos I have done so far:

The Procession of Shame by Charlie Watson on Vimeo.

This short film was a group project Directed by Charles Watson that I edited and helped collect audio for, it was done this year (2017) for an assignment. The premise for this short film was to re-create a scene from a famous film as closely as possible, we used "What We Do In The Shadows" for inspiration.


3.6 Final by Caitlyn Manderson on Vimeo.

This short film was the first video I ever really edited and is possibly my favourite of all my videos, it was made for an assessment in 2016 and is the main reason I wanted to continue editing further.
